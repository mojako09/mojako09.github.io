Safety Protocols & Guidelines

Cinema Safety Protocols

Temperature

Moviegoers must take a body temperature check before entering the cinema

Vaccination Card and Face Mask

Moviegoers must present an authentic vaccination card that shows the completion of two vaccine doses and must wear face mask at all times as IATF’s current ruling

Socially-distanced Seating

Theaters will implement “socially-distanced seating,” even for moviegoers who are entering together (two seats apart)

Foods and Drinks

Eating and drinking are allowed.

Sanitations and Air ways

Air supply is circulated in all cinemas, with hourly monitoring of CO2 levels. Deep cleaning of surfaces, chairs between screenings
